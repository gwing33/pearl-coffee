OUR PRODUCT

Our beans are Sourced Globally & Roasted Locally. Ours bags of coffee are made to order- which helps maintain high quality and guaranteed freshness for every batch. We rotate our coffee origins based on what is in season. We are taking you back to school and educating you on the worlds coffee, one cup at a time.

  • Gerald Leenerts is our design / marketing / taste-tester guru and co-owner. His favorite drinks include cold brew ice coffee, iced americano --anything to keep him nice and cool.

We believe that drinking coffee and that surrounding ourselves in the local community is what life should be about.

Our specialty is in light and medium roasted coffee from regions all over the world.
